Jalebi Recipe

Description

The south Asian variety is made by deep-frying maida flour

(plain flour or all-purpose flour) batter in pretzel or

circular shapes, which are then soaked in sugar syrup.

Jalebi is eaten with curd or rabri (in North India) along

with optional other flavors such as kewra (scented water).

Ingredients

  • Maida(flour)
  • Oil
  • Spices
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Saffron

Steps:-

  1. Take measured amounts of flour.
  2. Make a smooth batter.
  3. Add oil in a saucepan for deep-frying
  4. Before frying, prepare sugar-syrup in another saucepan
  5. Start making spiral shaped jalebis and cook for 5-10mins
  6. After they are golden brown, soak them in sugar-syrup
  7. Hot and crisp jalebis are ready!
