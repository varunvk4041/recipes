Biryani Recipe

Description

Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating among the royal khansamas of the

durbar of Old Delhi, under the Mughal Empire, during the late 16th century

of the then Mughal Court.

Ingredients

  • Chicken 500gm
  • Rice
  • Curd
  • Spices
  • Desi Ghee
  • Vegies

Steps:-

  1. Wash the meat and vegies properly.
  2. Cut the vegies and meat into appropriate sizes.
  3. Marinate the chicken for 2 hours
  4. After marination, cook it with spices
  5. Layer the cooked meat with rice and let it cook slowly.
  6. Tasty biryani is ready!
